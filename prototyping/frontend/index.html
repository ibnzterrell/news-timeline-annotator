The Timeline Annotator grabs a screentime graph from a Stanford Cable TV News Analyzer query, performs major event detection using screentime volume, constructs an event timeline summarization using news articles from the New York Times, and adds event annotations to the graph to help provide context.

Instructions: Visit the Stanford Cable TV News Analyzer, use it to make a query, and use "Copy URL" to copy and paste that query URL into the box above. Click the "Annotate" button.
